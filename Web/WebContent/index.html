Welcome to Arts Wellington

The Wellington Regional Art & Cultural Development Trust (Arts Wellington) is a charitable trust that provides networking opportunities, communication platforms, advocacy and capability building forums for our membership base. Our members are made up of most of the Greater Wellington region’s professional arts, culture and heritage organisations, arts service organisations and education institutions.  

Check out our weekly Opportunities E-bulletin newsletter.  Other key ongoing initiatives include the six-weekly Arts Partners, (a regular gathering of people working in arts marketing and administration), and bi-monthly Strategic Forums (addressing specific issues, and identified needs and opportunities in the sector). Arts Wellington also undertakes research on behalf of the sector, and advocates for the combined arts industry in Wellington.

In 2011 Arts Wellington completed the Arts Wellington Economic Impact Survey on behalf of members.

For any enquiries please contact info@artswellington.org.nz.
